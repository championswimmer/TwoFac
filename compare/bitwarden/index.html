What Is Bitwarden Authenticator?

Bitwarden offers two distinct 2FA solutions. The Bitwarden Authenticator is a free, standalone mobile app (Android and iOS) that generates TOTP codes independently — no Bitwarden account required.

The integrated authenticator lives inside the Bitwarden Password Manager and attaches TOTP codes directly to vault login items. This requires a Premium subscription ($10/year) or an organization plan.

Both Bitwarden products are open source. However, the standalone authenticator is mobile-only — no native desktop app, no CLI, no web interface, and no wearable support. The integrated authenticator is more fully featured but comes at a cost and requires a Bitwarden account.
